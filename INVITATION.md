I've been working on something called **Ledgr** — a free, open-source desktop app for tracking your budget and forecasting your finances. Think of it as a personal finance tool that actually respects your privacy: all your data stays on your machine, no accounts to sign up for, no cloud, no tracking.

You can import bank statements (OFX/CSV), categorize transactions automatically, set savings goals, track your net worth, manage investments, and even forecast your balances out months or years. It runs on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

It's still early days, so I'd really appreciate it if you gave it a spin and let me know what you think — what works, what's confusing, what's missing, all of it. Honest feedback is the most helpful kind.

Grab it here: https://github.com/jchilcher/ledgr-desktop/releases

Thanks!
